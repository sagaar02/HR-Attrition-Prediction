# HR-Attrition-Prediction

## 📌 Project Overview

Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations as it affects productivity, recruitment costs, and business continuity. This project uses Machine Learning to analyze employee data and predict whether an employee is likely to leave the organization. By identifying the key factors contributing to attrition, organizations can take proactive measures to improve employee retention and workforce management.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The primary objective of this project is to develop a predictive model that accurately classifies employees as likely to stay or leave the organization. The project also provides interactive visualizations through Power BI to help HR professionals understand attrition trends and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 📊 Dataset

* **Dataset:** IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance
* **Source:** Kaggle
* **Records:** 1,470 Employees
* **Features:** 35 Employee Attributes
* **Target Variable:** Attrition (Yes/No)

## 🚀 Project Workflow

### Phase 1 – Problem Definition

* Define the employee attrition problem.
* Identify business objectives and target variable.

### Phase 2 – Data Collection

* Load the IBM HR Analytics dataset.
* Explore the dataset structure and features.

### Phase 3 – Data Understanding & Cleaning

* Check for missing values.
* Remove duplicate records.
* Remove unnecessary columns.
* Verify data quality.

### Phase 4 – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Analyze employee attrition trends.
* Study department-wise attrition.
* Analyze overtime, salary, age, work-life balance, and job satisfaction.
* Create visualizations to identify business insights.

### Phase 5 – Feature Engineering & Data Preprocessing

* Remove irrelevant features.
* Encode categorical variables.
* Scale numerical features.
* Split the dataset into training and testing sets.

### Phase 6 – Model Development

* Train a Logistic Regression model.
* Generate employee attrition predictions.

### Phase 7 – Model Evaluation

Evaluate the model using:

* Accuracy
* Precision
* Recall
* F1-Score
* ROC-AUC Score
* Confusion Matrix

### Phase 8 – Dashboard Development

Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard containing:

* KPI Cards
* Department Analysis
* Overtime Analysis
* Salary Analysis
* Job Satisfaction Analysis
* Work-Life Balance Analysis
* Employee Attrition Insights

---

## 📈 Key Findings

* Employees working overtime are significantly more likely to leave the organization.
* Sales department records the highest employee attrition.
* Lower monthly income is associated with higher attrition.
* Employees with poor work-life balance have a higher likelihood of leaving.
* Lower job satisfaction contributes to increased employee turnover.
* Younger employees show a higher attrition tendency compared to older employees.
